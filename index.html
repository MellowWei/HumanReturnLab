AI 能看见我，
但不能替我，定义我。 AI can see me —
but it cannot define me, for me.

AI 正在扩展人的表达、理解、创造与行动。它能做的越来越多——正因如此，更该弄清：哪些事，仍然只能、也应当，由你亲自完成。Human Return Lab 用工作坊、对谈、研究与媒体，重新安放人的感受、判断、身体、关系与行动。 AI keeps extending how we express, understand, create, and act. It can do more and more — which is exactly why the question sharpens: what still can only, and should only, be done by you. Human Return Lab re-places the human — feeling, judgment, body, relation, action — through workshops, dialogues, research, and media.

返身宣言Manifesto

我们不反 AI，也不神化 AI。
我们只问：哪些，仍然只能由你亲自完成？ We are not against AI, nor do we worship it.
We ask only: what can be done by no one but you?

AI 正从工具变成一种新的能力环境。要紧的不只是它能做什么，而是它如何改变人理解自己、表达自己、判断现实、进入行动的方式。 AI is turning from a tool into a new environment of capability. What matters is not only what it can do, but how it reshapes the way we understand ourselves, speak, judge what's real, and step into action.

01 · 表达Expression AI 可以生成语言，不能替你拥有它。AI can generate language, not own it for you.

生成一句话，不等于拥有它。最终的选择、修改、承担与签字，仍然属于你。Generating a sentence is not the same as owning it. The final choice, the edit, the responsibility, the signature — still yours.

02 · 解释Interpretation AI 可以做镜子，不能做法官。AI can be a mirror, not a judge.

被 AI 说中，不等于被 AI 定义。命盘非判决，模型也一样——它给出映照，你保留解释权。Being read accurately is not being defined. A chart is not a verdict, and neither is a model — it reflects; you keep the right to interpret.

03 · 身体Embodiment AI 可以读取身体数据，不能替你亲自感受。AI can read your body's data, not feel it for you.

身体数据是线索，不是判决。先感受，再命名；先自己，再模型。Body data is a clue, not a verdict. Feel first, then name; self first, then model.

04 · 行动Agency AI 可以替你行动，不能替你划定边界。AI can act for you, not draw your limits.

能执行任务，不等于有权替你决定。授权、退出、停止与责任，必须始终可追踪、可撤回。Executing a task is not the authority to decide for you. Grant, exit, stop, and responsibility must stay traceable — and revocable.

AI时代，人如何返回自身？How does a person return to themselves in the age of AI?

真正好的 AI，不是让人更像机器，而是帮助人更清醒地成为人。 Good AI doesn't make people more like machines — it helps people become, more clearly, themselves.
持续回到自己 —— 一次次被带走，又一次次回来。 Keep returning to yourself — carried off again and again, and coming back again and again.
