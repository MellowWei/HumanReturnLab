AI 正在成为新的能力环境：它帮助我们表达、理解、创造和行动，也开始进入我们的自我解释、身体数据、亲密关系与现实权限。Human Return Lab 不是教人远离 AI，而是共同练习——如何使用 AI，却不交出自己。AI is becoming an environment of capability: extending expression, understanding, creation, and action while entering self-interpretation, bodily data, intimate relations, and real-world permissions. Human Return Lab is not about leaving AI, but learning to use it without handing ourselves over.

为什么是现在Why now

AI 正在发生四次位置变化。AI is changing position in four ways.

真正需要讨论的，不再只是模型“聪不聪明”，而是它正在进入人的哪一层，以及人是否仍保留回收解释、判断与行动的能力。The central issue is no longer only how intelligent a model is, but which layer of human life it enters—and whether people can still reclaim interpretation, judgment, and action.

SHIFT 01

从工具到环境Tool → Environment

AI 不只完成任务，也在重组我们看见、表达、搜索和相信的方式。AI does not only finish tasks; it reorganizes how we see, express, search, and believe.

SHIFT 02

从回答到解释Answer → Interpretation

它开始解释情绪、关系、能力与人格。问题从“答得对吗”升级为：谁有权定义我？It interprets emotion, relation, ability, and personality. The question becomes: who gets to define me?

SHIFT 03

从聊天到行动Chat → Action

Agent 接入邮箱、日历、代码、支付和设备后，AI 获得现实权限。授权与停止成为核心。Once agents connect to email, calendars, code, payments, and devices, authorization and stopping become central.

SHIFT 04

从软件到基础设施Software → Infrastructure

AI 的身体是芯片、电力、数据中心、劳动力与全球供应链。它也是资源和分配问题。AI's body is chips, power, data centers, labor, and supply chains. It is also a question of resources and distribution.

Human Return Framework

它不是一句口号，而是一套可操作的返回系统。Not a slogan—a usable system of return.

六个生活领域告诉我们 AI 进入哪里；四个返身动作告诉我们怎么回来；六项主体权利把个人练习连接到产品设计与公共治理。Six domains locate where AI enters; four practices show how to return; six rights connect personal practice to product design and public governance.

返回自身，不是摆脱外部影响；而是知道什么进入了我、进入了哪里，并决定它是否成为我。Returning to oneself is not escaping influence. It is knowing what entered me, where it entered, and deciding whether it becomes part of me.

核心判断：AI 使用之后，我是否更能感受、判断并行动？还是只能继续依赖它解释、批准和推动我？Core test: after using AI, am I more able to feel, judge, and act—or more dependent on it to interpret, approve, and move me?

六个返回Six Returns

同一个问题，从六个真实入口展开。One question, six lived entrances.

RETURN 01

返回感受Feeling

感质主权 · 情绪标签 · AI 情绪分析 · 内在体验qualia sovereignty · affect labels · inner experience

AI 可以描述我的感受，但不能替我亲自感受。AI may describe my feeling, not feel it for me.

RETURN 02

返回判断Judgment

答案审计 · 价值排序 · 不确定性 · 判断签名answer audit · values · uncertainty · signature

AI 可以给答案，但最后必须由我签字。AI may answer, but I must sign.

RETURN 03

返回表达Expression

作者性 · 人机共创 · 语言主权 · 作品责任authorship · co-creation · voice · responsibility

AI 可以给我语言，但不能替我拥有意义。AI may offer language, not own its meaning.

RETURN 04

返回身体Body

可穿戴 · 身体数据 · 情绪识别 · 脑机接口wearables · body data · affect recognition · BCI

身体数据是线索，不是对我的最终判决。Body data is a clue, not a verdict.

RETURN 05

返回关系Relation

AI 陪伴 · 被理解感 · 依赖 · 真实共在companionship · understanding · dependence · presence

AI 可以参与关系，但不能替我承担关系。AI may enter relation, not bear it for me.

RETURN 06

返回行动Action

Agent · 工具权限 · 行动发起权 · 停止机制agents · permissions · initiation · stopping

AI 可以执行，但行动方向必须仍由我发出。AI may execute; direction must still come from me.

参与式研究底线Research safeguards

“研究产出”不能成为对参与者经验的再次攫取。Research must not become another extraction of participants' experience.

Human Return Audit

AI 已经进入你生活的哪一层？Which layer of your life has AI entered?

这不是心理测评，也不判断你“会不会正确使用 AI”。它只帮你看见：哪里是辅助，哪里正在变成替代。Not a psychological test and not a score of “correct AI use.” It simply reveals where assistance may be turning into replacement.

04

05
